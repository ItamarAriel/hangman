# Hangman

A simple Hangman game.

Intended for a 2-player local experience.

## Rules

* One player chooses a word or phrase.
* The other player tries to guess the word/phrase by guessing one letter at a time.
* If the guess is correct, all occurrences of that letter are revealed.
* If the guess is wrong, the guessing player loses a life.
* If the guessing player runs out of lives, the player who chose the word/phrase wins.
* Otherwise, the player who guessed the word/phrase wins.
